Hambeerguesa

Burgers & Beer

Welcome to Hambeerguesa! The No. 1 place for the burger and beer lover.

We have a burning passion for making the juiciest and sexiest burgers as well as picking out the coldest and most tasteful beers on this planet to accompany them.

We believe that good burgers with good beer is every humans right and we want to give people that special experience they are missing.

At Hambeerguesa we don't only serve food and drinks, but we offer an experience. Wether you're a burger connoiseur or sommelier, or just your average Joe that enjoys some sliders with beer, we believe that we can offer the greatest combinations of food and drinks based on your preferences.

All our servers are experienced sommeliers with a special knack for beers as well as people. Let us get to know you and we will find the best combinations suited for you.

We have a wide variety of meats, buns, secondaries, as well as a plethora of beers. IPA, APA, Lager, Unfiltered, Stout, even Vegetable beer. You name it, we have it!
